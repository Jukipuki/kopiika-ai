## Technique Execution Results

### Cross-Pollination (10 domain-pair crossings)

**[Finance #1]**: Cumulative Financial Health Record
_Concept_: Every transaction feeds into a growing personal financial profile. AI detects impulse categories, seasonal spending spikes, recurring leaks — gets progressively smarter about you specifically.
_Novelty_: Not a dashboard that shows what happened — a system that learns and predicts your behavior.

**[Finance #2]**: Financial Triage Agent
_Concept_: AI scans financial data and triages issues by severity — "Subscription creep bleeding $180/month (urgent)" vs. "Could optimize grocery spending by ~$30/month (low priority)."
_Novelty_: Most finance apps show everything equally. This tells you what to fix first based on impact.

**[Finance #3]**: Financial Mise en Place
_Concept_: Before each month, AI reviews upcoming obligations + historical patterns to create a "prep station" — what's committed, discretionary budget, likely surprises.
_Novelty_: Proactive "prep" rather than reactive "review." Uses accumulated history to predict.

**[Fitness #1]**: Fitness Portfolio Tracker
_Concept_: Log exercises, AI builds cumulative training balance profile. Compares actual training distribution against goals, flags meaningful imbalances monthly, not daily.
_Novelty_: Tracks whether what you did is actually moving you toward your goal.

**[Fitness #2]**: AI Training Protocol Engine *(User favorite)*
_Concept_: Define a goal, AI generates multi-week training protocol via RAG. Log actual workouts, AI compares protocol vs. reality and adjusts.
_Novelty_: Living protocol that adapts based on accumulated data, not a static plan generator.

**[Fitness #3]**: Sensor-Augmented Training Logger
_Concept_: Use phone accelerometer, HR strap, or Arduino sensors to auto-capture exercise data. AI processes raw sensor streams into structured workout logs.
_Novelty_: Bridges electronics hobby with fitness. Great data processing pipeline demo.

**[CrossFit-Cook #1]**: Meal-as-Fuel Planner
_Concept_: Log workouts AND meals. AI correlates eating habits with performance and suggests meals based on upcoming training. RAG over recipe + nutrition knowledge base.
_Novelty_: Connects your actual training log to your actual eating habits.

**[Cook-Finance #1]**: Smart Grocery & Meal Budget Optimizer
_Concept_: Track grocery spending + meals made. AI calculates real cost-per-meal, identifies waste, suggests budget-efficient recipes.
_Novelty_: Data-driven waste reduction and cost-per-nutrition optimization.

**[Cook-Health #1]**: Personal Dietary Protocol Engine *(User favorite)*
_Concept_: Input health goals, preferences, allergies. AI creates personalized dietary protocol via RAG over nutritional science. Tracks adherence and adjusts.
_Novelty_: Knows your taste, cooking skill, pantry, AND health targets.

**[Cook-Electronics #1]**: Smart Kitchen Inventory & Recipe Agent *(User favorite)*
_Concept_: Snap fridge/pantry photo or scan receipts. Vision AI maintains inventory. Recipe agent queries inventory + preference history + recipe RAG.
_Novelty_: Vision AI + RAG + accumulated preference data. Remembers pantry and waste patterns.

**[Finance-Electronics #1]**: Passive Financial Intelligence *(User favorite)*
_Concept_: AI agents auto-ingest bank statements (CSV/PDF), extract and categorize transactions, detect patterns. Multi-agent pipeline: Ingestion -> Categorization -> Pattern Detection -> Insight.
_Novelty_: Multi-agent pipeline showcase. Gets smarter at categorizing your specific merchants.

**[Cook-Finance #2]**: Recipe ROI Tracker
_Concept_: Track recipes tried, time, cost, enjoyment rating. AI calculates "recipe ROI" and prioritizes suggestions by your sweet spot.
_Novelty_: Gamifies recipe exploration with financial mindset.

**[Triple #1]**: Health-Finance-Fitness Unified Dashboard
_Concept_: Gym performance, nutrition spending, health metrics in one AI dashboard. Finds cross-domain correlations.
_Novelty_: No app connects these three with longitudinal personal data.

### SCAMPER Method

**[SCAMPER-S #1]**: Statement-Upload Financial Intelligence
_Concept_: Drag-and-drop bank statements (CSV/PDF). Multi-agent pipeline parses -> categorizes -> detects patterns -> generates triage + monthly prep briefing. No bank API needed.
_Novelty_: Solves "I have a spreadsheet but never analyze it" with zero API integration complexity.

**[SCAMPER-S #2]**: Manual-Log Training Protocol Engine
_Concept_: Same adaptive protocol engine but input is simple manual logging or voice-to-structured-data. AI value is in understanding data, not capturing it.
_Novelty_: Removes hardest technical challenge (CV) while keeping smartest part (adaptive protocols).

**[SCAMPER-C #1]**: Integrated Training & Nutrition Protocol Engine
_Concept_: Training Agent manages workout plan; Nutrition Agent manages meal plan. They collaborate: heavy squat day -> high-carb dinner suggestion. Multi-agent orchestration showcase.
_Novelty_: Two specialized agents collaborating. Nutrition and training deeply connected but every app treats them separately.

**[SCAMPER-C #2]**: Budget-Aware Kitchen Agent
_Concept_: Kitchen inventory agent feeds into finance agent. Tracks food waste, optimizes grocery spending through meal suggestions.
_Novelty_: Connects the money side with the food side.

**[SCAMPER-A #1]**: Spaced Progression Training
_Concept_: Apply spaced repetition from language learning to progressive overload scheduling. AI tracks when you last increased weight/reps and schedules next push at optimal interval.
_Novelty_: Proven learning science algorithm applied to physical training. Needs full history.

**[SCAMPER-M #1]**: Financial Literacy Coach *(User favorite)*
_Concept_: Every insight comes with plain-language explanation layer. RAG over financial literacy corpus + your personal data = contextual financial education.
_Novelty_: Transforms finance tool into personalized education system.

**[SCAMPER-P #1]**: Personal Health Intake Agent
_Concept_: AI interviews you about symptoms/history before doctor visits. Builds cumulative health profile, generates structured summary to bring to appointments.
_Novelty_: Uses healthcare domain expertise. Same cumulative profile + protocol pattern applied to healthcare.

**[SCAMPER-E #1]**: Voice-First Training Coach *(User favorite)*
_Concept_: No complex UI. Talk to AI between sets. It logs, compares to protocol, responds with guidance. Database and RAG work behind the scenes.
_Novelty_: Eliminates logging friction. UI is a chat. Value is in cumulative data + intelligent protocol.

### Constraint Mapping

#### Family A: Smart Finance — Constraint Analysis

| Constraint | Hard/Soft | Analysis |
|---|---|---|
| Handling sensitive financial data | Soft | Capstone = local data, no regulatory burden. Use sample/synthetic for demos. |
| Bank API integrations | Eliminated | CSV/PDF upload instead. |
| Transaction categorization accuracy | Soft | LLMs good at this. Gets better with correction history. |
| Financial advice liability | Soft | Frame as "insights and education." Non-issue for capstone. |
| RAG knowledge base for financial literacy | Soft | Plenty of open-source financial literacy content. |
| Multi-agent pipeline complexity | Medium | 4-5 agents ambitious but achievable. Start with 2-3 for MVP. |
| Frontend complexity | Soft | Dashboard + charts + chat. Well-trodden ground. |
| Demo-ability | Hard (positive) | Upload statement, watch agents process, see insights. Very visual. |

**Verdict: Very few hard constraints. Highly buildable.**

#### Family B: Smart Training — Constraint Analysis

| Constraint | Hard/Soft | Analysis |
|---|---|---|
| Computer vision for form tracking | Eliminated | Manual/voice logging instead. |
| Exercise science knowledge base | Soft | Plenty of open content for RAG. |
| Voice input processing | Medium | STT solved (Whisper). Structured extraction is a good LLM task. |
| Adaptive protocol generation | Medium | Core AI challenge — comparing planned vs. actual, detecting plateaus. |
| Nutrition integration | Soft (scope risk) | Defer to V2. MVP works without it. |
| Biometric/IoT sensors | Deferred | Cool but not needed for MVP. |
| Frontend complexity | Medium | Logging UI + protocol view + progress charts. More interactive. |
| Demo-ability | Hard (positive) | Log workouts, show AI adapting protocol. Compelling before/after. |

**Verdict: Buildable but adaptive protocol engine is the hard intellectual challenge.**

### GenAI Architecture

**Multi-Agent Pipeline:**
1. **Ingestion Agent** — Parses uploaded CSV/PDF bank statements, extracts raw transactions
2. **Categorization Agent** — Classifies transactions (food, transport, subscriptions, etc.), learns from user corrections
3. **Pattern Detection Agent** — Analyzes cumulative history for trends, anomalies, recurring patterns
4. **Triage Agent** — Prioritizes findings by financial impact severity
5. **Education Agent** — Generates plain-language explanations using RAG over financial literacy knowledge base

**Additional GenAI Patterns:**
- **RAG**: Financial literacy corpus for contextual education
- **Tool Use**: Database queries, calculation tools, categorization functions
- **Structured Workflows**: Upload -> parse -> categorize -> analyze -> triage -> educate -> present
- **Conditional Logic**: Different insight paths based on data patterns (new user vs. returning, healthy vs. concerning trends)

### MVP Feature Scope (Recommended V1)

**Must Have:**
- CSV/PDF statement upload and parsing
- Transaction categorization (AI + user correction)
- Cumulative spending profile in database
- Basic pattern detection (top categories, month-over-month trends)
- Triage-ranked insights ("fix this first")
- Plain-language explanations for each insight (RAG)
- Dashboard UI with spending charts + insight cards

**Nice to Have (V1 stretch):**
- Monthly "mise en place" prep briefing
- Budget goal setting and tracking
- Subscription detection and tracking

**V2 (Future):**
- Investment recommendations
- Multi-account support
- Predictive spending forecasts
- Natural language chat interface for querying your data
