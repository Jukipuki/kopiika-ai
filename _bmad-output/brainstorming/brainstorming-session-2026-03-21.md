# Brainstorming Session Results

**Facilitator:** Oleh
**Date:** 2026-03-21

## Session Overview

**Topic:** Product/brand naming for Smart Financial Intelligence & Literacy Coach
**Goals:** Deep exploration of Kopiika (Копійка) direction with variations + shortlist of 3-5 strong name candidates with rationale

### Session Setup

- Ukrainian-first AI-powered financial literacy platform
- Must resonate with Ukrainian market (9.88M+ Monobank users)
- Approachable for financially anxious users yet credible for power users
- Education-first positioning, not just another fintech dashboard
- Works as an app name (short, memorable, easy to type/search)
- Selected approach: AI-Recommended Techniques

## Technique Selection

**Approach:** AI-Recommended Techniques
**Analysis Context:** Product/brand naming with focus on deep Kopiika exploration + shortlist

**Recommended Techniques:**

- **SCAMPER Method:** Systematically explore Kopiika through 7 creative lenses (Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other uses, Eliminate, Reverse)
- **Cross-Pollination:** Break free from Kopiika-centric thinking by pulling naming patterns from education, Ukrainian consumer brands, and unexpected domains
- **Six Thinking Hats:** Multi-dimensional evaluation of top candidates through Facts, Emotions, Benefits, Risks, Creativity, and Process lenses

**AI Rationale:** User had a strong lead candidate (Kopiika) requiring both deep stress-testing and broad alternative exploration, followed by structured evaluation to reach a confident final decision.

## Technique Execution Results

### Phase 1: SCAMPER Method — Kopiika Deep Dive

**Interactive Focus:** Systematically twisting Kopiika through all 7 SCAMPER lenses

**Ideas Generated:**

1. **Kopійка AI** — AI suffix for tech positioning; functional but potentially generic
2. **Kopiiko** (Копійко) — Neutral/masculine ending; companion feel but loses coin meaning
3. **Kopik** (Копік) — Shortened, punchy; app-friendly but loses cultural weight
4. **Hroshy** (Гроші) — Broader "money" concept; loses "attention to detail" implication
5. **kōpiika** — Macron styling for visual distinction; keeps word intact, adds brand identity
6. **KopiikaCoach** — Explicit education positioning; too long for app icon
7. **Kopiika+** — Plus suffix signals premium; elegant but generic pattern
8. **MyKopiika** (МояКопійка) — Personal ownership; overused "My[X]" pattern
9. **KopiikaRozum** (КопійкаРозум) — "Penny intelligence"; encodes both concepts but too long
10. **Kopiika Mentor** — Education relationship positioning; mixes Ukrainian and English
11. **KOPIIKA** — All-caps brand identity; bold but loses warmth
12. **Kopiika as verb** — "Копійкувати" works grammatically in Ukrainian; strong brand signal
13. **Kopii** (Копії) — Stripped to two syllables; means "copies" — disqualified
14. **Akiipok** — Pure reversal; confirms name's power is in recognition

**Key Breakthrough:** Kopiika is remarkably resilient to modification — its strength IS the unmodified cultural word. The macron styling (kōpiika) emerged as the winning approach: distinctive without altering the word itself.

**User Creative Insight:** Oleh consistently rejected modifications that diluted cultural authenticity, revealing that completeness and genuine Ukrainian identity are non-negotiable brand values.

### Phase 2: Cross-Pollination — Breaking Free from Kopiika Gravity

**User Taste Profile Discovered:**

- Brilliant (aspirational) — likes naming the outcome, not the tool
- Mono — ultra-short, clean, modern, premium through simplicity
- Inzhur — Ukrainian phonetic adaptation of English concept, feels native yet international
- Pattern: **short, clean, modern, meaningful — no fluff**

**Ideas Generated:**

18. **Fintik** (Фінтік) — "Фін" + "тік"; playful but too casual for financial education

23. **Kashl** (Кешл) — Ukrainianized "cash"; follows Inzhur pattern but "шл" cluster sounds harsh
24. **Fainans** (Файнанс) — Ukrainianized "finance"; too literal
25. **Spendr** (Спендр) — Ukrainianized "spend" + tech suffix; easy to understand but wrong semantics

**Key Breakthrough:** Insight #32 — Mono doesn't explain itself in its name, it earns meaning through experience. kōpiika can follow the same playbook. The name carries cultural weight; the subtitle/descriptor carries functional clarity.

### Phase 3: Six Thinking Hats — Multi-Dimensional Evaluation

**Candidates Evaluated:** kōpiika, Kopiika AI, Spendr, Kopiika (plain)

**White Hat (Facts):**
- kōpiika macron creates URL/typing friction — mitigated by owning plain "kopiika" for digital
- "Kopiika AI" has SEO advantage but "AI" suffix becoming commodity
- Spendr has best international legibility but wrong semantic fit
- Plain Kopiika competes with dictionary results in search

**Red Hat (Emotions):**
- kōpiika feels premium, intentional, warm — signals "we thought about every detail"
- Kopiika AI feels functional but commodity
- Spendr feels young/startup-y but emotionally about outflow not learning
- Plain Kopiika is honest but undifferentiated

**Yellow Hat (Benefits):**
- kōpiika macron becomes distinctive brand asset, instantly recognizable
- Cultural pride positioning: "We're Ukrainian-first and proud of it"
- Storytelling gold: "Every kōpiika matters" = entire product philosophy
- Connects to proverb "копійка гривню береже" (a penny saves the hryvnia)

**Black Hat (Risks):**
- Macron typing friction → mitigated by dual domain strategy
- "Penny" could read as cheap → mitigated by premium visual branding
- Non-Ukrainian pronunciation risk → acceptable for Ukrainian-first market

**Green Hat (Creative Possibilities):**
- Macron "ō" becomes visual motif — safety net, horizon, growth line
- Could animate in app as Financial Health Score progress indicator
- Marketing: "The line above ordinary"
- Brand architecture: kōpiika (emotional) + AI Financial Coach (functional descriptor)

**Blue Hat (Scoring):**

## Idea Organization and Prioritization

### Final Shortlist (Ranked)

**1. kōpiika (Primary Brand — SELECTED)**
- Culturally authentic Ukrainian word every user recognizes instantly
- Macron styling creates distinctive, premium brand identity
- Storytelling-ready: "Every kōpiika matters"
- Resilient through all SCAMPER stress-testing — the word's strength is its completeness

**2. Kopiika AI (Functional Descriptor)**
- Retained for app store listings and SEO contexts: "kopiika — AI Financial Coach"
- Not the brand name, but the positioning clarifier

**3. Spendr (Parked — International Future)**
- Easy to understand, startup-friendly, international appeal
- Wrong semantics for education-first product, but valid for future brand extension consideration

**4. Varto (Honorable Mention)**
- "Worth it" — strong aspirational positioning, ultra-short
- Could serve as campaign language: "Varto спробувати kōpiika"

**5. Seivy (Honorable Mention)**
- "Savvy" ukrainianized — describes the user outcome perfectly
- Could serve as feature naming or campaign language
