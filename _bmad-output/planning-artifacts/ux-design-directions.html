Full-Focus Card Stack

Each card commands full attention. Triage severity (coral/amber/sage) is visible at a glance. Progressive disclosure lets users go as deep as they want.

Trust-First Onboarding

No lengthy setup, no tutorial carousel. One screen, one action: upload your file. Trust signals (encryption, no credentials, delete anytime) are visible but not dominant.

  • "See what your money is trying to tell you" — curiosity-driven, not feature-driven
  • Single action: tap the upload zone, select file, done
  • Trust signals below the fold — present but not anxious
  • Supported formats mentioned subtly in the upload hint
  • No account creation wall before first upload (freemium discovery)
  • Warm, inviting visual tone — not clinical bank interface
Direction 7

Error States — Humor Disarms Anxiety

When things go wrong, the product stays lighthearted and helpful. Never clinical, never alarming.

🤔
Hmm, can't read this one
Our AI got confused by your file format. Try exporting a fresh CSV from Monobank?
😴
Nothing new to report
This statement overlaps with data we already have. Upload a new month to get fresh insights!
🔧
We tripped over something
Our AI had a hiccup processing your data. We've been notified and are looking into it. Mind trying again?

Error State Principles

  • Every error has an emoji — warmth before words
  • Headlines are conversational, not technical ("Hmm, can't read this" not "Parse Error 422")
  • Body text explains what happened AND what to do next
  • Single clear action button — never leave the user stuck
  • Tone is a smart friend apologizing, not a system reporting a failure
  • Never blame the user ("your file is invalid") — always take responsibility ("our AI got confused")
